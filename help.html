How to Send Money
  1. Click on "Send Money" button
  2. Enter recipient mobile number
  3. Enter the amount you want to send
  4. Review and confirm the transaction
How to Check Balance
  1. Click on "Check Balance" button
  2. Your current balance will be displayed
  3. Click "Refresh" to update the balance
Security Tips
  • Never share your OTP with anyone
  • Always verify recipient details before sending money
  • Logout when you finish using the app
  • Keep your mobile number secure
